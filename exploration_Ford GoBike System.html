by Serena Zatara¶

Preliminary Wrangling¶

This document will explore the Ford GoBike's trip data for public containing approximately 295854 bike rides for January 2020. "With a Ford GoBike membership, you get the first 45 minutes free, not just the first 30." With this report this will be an analysis of membership/subscriber usage and the duration of usuage for the launch of Ford GoBike now under Baywheels for 2020.

What is the structure of your dataset?¶

Looking at the January 2020 data there are 295,854 bike rides for the San Francisco Bay area. The data contains the following information: Trip Duration- start and end times Station bikes start and end with names Bike Ids User Type

The dataframe was updated to further include in addtion to the start and end date columns breaking this data for further analysis into Month and Weekday

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

Looking through the data I plan to provide a report for bike users for 2020 to help provide a look at the start for the first month trends for future forecasting. This will include looking into the following:

  1. Bike demand for weekday for the first month report, and for furutre schedule planning
  2. The current duration of time spent on bikes
  3. The users type- subscriber vs customer
  4. Top most popular start and end locations

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

I feel Friday through Sunday maybe the strongest days bikes are being used. Validating if our current subscribers are using bikes more or do subscription oppurtunties need to be reviewed on presentation. From the data I expect to see Ford GoBike's strongest locations.
